## DenkKern Platform

### Core Features

**Decision Intelligence Engine**
Structured decision frameworks embedded in your operations. Compare options systematically, model outcomes, and evaluate trade-offs—all in one system.

**Context Aggregation**
Connect any data source: ERP, MES, external APIs, real-time sensors. DenkKern brings together fragmented information without manual data prep.

**Scenario Comparison**
Evaluate multiple possible actions in parallel. Understand confidence levels, second-order effects, and constraints for each option.

**Transparent Reasoning**
Every recommendation includes the reasoning behind it. See what factors influence the decision, which constraints are binding, and why this option ranks first.

**Learning Loop**
Capture outcomes from every decision. Improve recommendations based on what actually happened in production, not just what the model predicted.

**Human Approval Workflow**
Recommendations flow to decision makers for approval. DenkKern surfaces the evidence. Humans make the call.

### How It Works

1. **Connect Your Data** - Integrate with ERP, MES, CRM, external systems
2. **Define Decisions** - Set up decision types your organization faces regularly
3. **Configure Options** - Specify what options are possible, what constraints apply
4. **Get Recommendations** - DenkKern evaluates all options and recommends the best path
5. **Approve & Execute** - Review reasoning, approve action, execute decision
6. **Learn & Improve** - Capture outcomes, improve future recommendations

### Platform Components

- **Decision Inbox** – Central dashboard for decisions requiring attention
- **Scenario Workbench** – Deep-dive tool for complex multi-option analysis
- **Historical Dashboard** – View past decisions, outcomes, and lessons learned
- **Admin Console** – Configure integrations, define decision types, manage users
- **Approval Workflow** – Route recommendations to appropriate decision makers
- **API** – Embed decision intelligence in any application

### FAQ

**What's your pricing model?**
We're working with pilot customers on customized pricing. Reach out to discuss your specific needs.

**When will DenkKern be generally available?**
We're currently in pilot phase. If you're interested in early access, let's talk.

**What data do you need to integrate?**
It depends on the decision type, but typically we connect to your ERP, MES, or other operational systems. More details during your demo.

**Do you replace my existing systems?**
No. DenkKern augments your existing stack. It connects to your data, but doesn't replace your ERP, BI, or other tools.

**Is DenkKern suitable for mid-market companies?**
Yes. If you have complex operational decisions and enough data volume to make recommendation engines useful, DenkKern applies.

## Industry Solutions

DenkKern solves decision intelligence problems across industries. Each vertical has unique constraints and decision types.

### Manufacturing

**Key Decisions:**
- Production scheduling and line balancing
- Supplier selection and demand allocation
- Inventory allocation across facilities
- Quality response and rework decisions
- Equipment maintenance scheduling

**Why DenkKern:**
Combine real-time production data, inventory levels, supplier availability, and quality metrics. Evaluate options against production capacity, cost, and delivery commitments. Learn from past scheduling decisions.

### Supply Chain & Logistics

**Key Decisions:**
- Carrier selection and routing
- Inventory positioning
- Demand planning and allocation
- Supplier contingency decisions
- Network optimization

**Why DenkKern:**
Model supply chain decisions against service levels, cost, and risk. Combine internal demand signals with external market data and supplier status. Evaluate multiple routing and sourcing options simultaneously.

### Procurement

**Key Decisions:**
- Vendor selection and RFQ evaluation
- Contract negotiation terms
- Category strategy and sourcing
- Spend optimization
- Risk assessment and mitigation

**Why DenkKern:**
Score vendors against price, quality, risk, and strategic fit. Compare procurement strategies for different categories. Learn from past vendor performance to improve future selections.

### Healthcare Operations

**Key Decisions:**
- Staff scheduling and allocation
- Resource planning
- Equipment maintenance
- Capacity decisions
- Emergency response protocols

**Why DenkKern:**
Optimize staffing against patient demand, skill requirements, and labor regulations. Model capacity decisions against throughput and quality. Compare scheduling strategies.

### Energy & Infrastructure

**Key Decisions:**
- Maintenance scheduling
- Grid optimization and load balancing
- Asset retirement and replacement
- Emergency response decisions
- Efficiency optimization

**Why DenkKern:**
Model long-term infrastructure decisions with multiple constraints. Evaluate maintenance timing against reliability, cost, and operational impact. Learn from past incidents to improve response protocols.

### Operations (General)

DenkKern applies to any operational domain where decisions are:
- **Complex** – Multiple variables, constraints, and dependencies
- **Recurrent** – The same decision type happens repeatedly
- **Time-sensitive** – Speed matters as much as quality
- **High-impact** – Decisions affect revenue, cost, or risk significantly
